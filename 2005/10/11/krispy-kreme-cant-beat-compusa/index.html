Krispy Kreme can't beat CompUSA

  • asides
You can almost smell the aroma of fresh plastic hot off the assembly line!

Source: First look: Xbox 360 accessory packaging - Joystiq - www.joystiq.com

Oh wait, I'm not the only one—there's at least one other, and I'm marrying her.
