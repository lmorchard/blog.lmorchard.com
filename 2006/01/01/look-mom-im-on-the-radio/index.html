Look, Mom, I'm on the radio!

  • asides

This past Thursday night, I was asked to dial in as a guest on Computer Outlook, a radio talk show hosted by John Iasiuolo out of Las Vegas, Nevada. We rambled on a bit about Hacking RSS and Atom and my experiences in writing it.

To hear the show, you can check out the audio archives of the show or even subscribe to it as a podcast. Not having lived in the broadcast area, I hadn't heard of this show before—but they seem to have a lot of fun on it, and I'm tuned in now via iTunes.

It's the first time I've been asked to take part in something like this, so I was a bit bewildered by the whole event. I tend to feel a bit stymied on conference calls, so I kept worrying whether I was talking too much or too little. I haven't had a chance to listen to the whole show yet and squirm at the sound of my own cell-phone mangled voice, but it was a very cool experience!
