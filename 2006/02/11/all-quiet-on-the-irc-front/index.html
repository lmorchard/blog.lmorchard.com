All quiet on the IRC front

  • asides

It's been awhile since I was last very active on IRC. For awhile, you could find me squatting in #joiito along with a smattering of other channels. Needing focus at work during the day, and for writing during the night, I just kind of dropped off the face of the networks.

Thanks to dircproxy and a machine with a very high uptime, though, my nick kind of lingered zombie-like. The lights were on, but I wasn't home. I've dropped in from time to time over the past few months, but things always seem dead compared to when I was frequenting as a regular. Maybe the party moved on and I just didn't follow it. These things seem to go in waves.
