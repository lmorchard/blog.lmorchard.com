Sync My Bookmarks No Longer!

So, I downloaded Safari 1.0 yesterday and was very pleased to notice a new checkbox option on the Bookmarks section of the preferences.

Notice the off state of the checkbox. Goodbye, bookmark syncing, I hardly wanted to know ya.
