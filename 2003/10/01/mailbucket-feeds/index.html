Mailbucket syndication feeds

Just discovered Tom Dyson's Mailbucket.org, and started playing around with signing up mailing lists for feeds. It's simple, send an email to foo@mailbucket.org, then check http://mailbucket.org/foo.xml for an RSS feed. I was tinkering with something like it last week, using Postfix, Mail::Audit, and blosxom -- but hey, if someone else has done it, I'll just go use theirs! :)
