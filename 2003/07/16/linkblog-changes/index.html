Changes in my link blogging

You might notice a sudden rise in link-quote-comment entries around here, depending on how well this works for my lazy self. I just threw together a quick bookmarklet and aggregator-integrated posting hack for myself, hoping it will be as easy as BookmarkBlogger for noting down URLs of interest throughout the day. Nothing revolutionary, just slightly new for me in daily use.

But, I was starting to wish that I could provide a little more info around my posted links, such as why I was sharing the link and from where I found it. So, I'll be trying a slightly different approach. Let me know if it gets annoying.
